About Our Lab

Founded in 2015, our laboratory is dedicated to advancing quantum computing technologies and their applications. Our interdisciplinary team combines expertise in physics, computer science, and mathematics to tackle the most challenging problems in quantum information science.

We collaborate with academic institutions and industry partners worldwide to accelerate the development and adoption of quantum technologies. Our research focuses on both fundamental science and practical implementations, with the goal of building robust, scalable quantum computing systems.

Our research spans multiple areas of quantum information science, with a focus on developing practical quantum technologies.
