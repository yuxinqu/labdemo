Our Team

Meet the talented researchers and students who make our laboratory's work possible.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Professor of Quantum Computing

Dr. Johnson leads our research on quantum algorithms and their applications. She received her PhD from MIT and has published over 50 papers in top quantum computing journals. Her research focuses on developing practical quantum algorithms for solving complex problems in cryptography and optimization.
